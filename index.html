A Powerful and Extendable 2D Game/Graphics Framework

Get Started View Source

Features:

  • Easily extendable
  • Object-based
  • Mobile and cross-browser support
  • Mouse/touch, keyboard and custom events
  • Physics with advanced collision detection
  • Sound
  • Cameras
  • Images
  • Sprites (with animations)
  • Shape primitive rendering
  • Multiple simultaneous stages
  • Collections
  • And much more!

G also has a few nifty plugins, including integration with the Box2D and Chipmunk Physics engines. It is also very easy to extend so that you can make your own custom plugins!
